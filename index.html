***Warning this game involves Mild violence with with fictional characters involving the use of firearms. Parental discretion is advised.*** Look alive! Be ready to use your mouse to aim and shoot(*!*LeftClick*!*). You will have a certain amount time to hit your target, or the enemies fire back.
